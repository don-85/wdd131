Site Name: RapidStream Adventures

Reason for selection: The name 'RapidStream Adventures' was chosen to reflect the exciting, fast-paced, and adventure-filled experiences we offer with white-water rafting and other outdoor activities. It captures the essence of thrilling outdoor fun in a natural environment.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the RapidStream Adventures website is to provide information about our thrilling white-water rafting and adventure services. The site includes detailed information about available rafting packages, safety guidelines, and booking forms for adventure seekers looking for an unforgettable outdoor experience.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Question: What are the safety measures for white-water rafting?

Answer: Our site will provide detailed safety guidelines, including gear recommendations, required physical fitness, and instructions on rafting safety.

Scenario 2

Question: How do I book a rafting adventure for a group?

Answer: We will include an easy-to-use booking form with options for group bookings and custom adventure packages.

Typography

Primary Font: 'Arial' is used for the body text for its clean and readable style.

Heading Font: 'Georgia' is applied to all headings to give a classic, elegant feel to important sections.
